What is Korea?

South Korea, officially the Republic of Korea, is a country in East Asia, located on the southern half of the Korean Peninsula. Known for its rich culture, South Korea is home to modern cities, traditional palaces, and the Korean Wave (Hallyu), which has made its entertainment, music, and fashion famous worldwide. Visitors can experience stunning natural landscapes, ancient temples, and delicious cuisine.
